    Flexible Learning Options: Choose from In-Person, Online, and Blended Learning Formats to Fit Your Schedule and Learning Style.

    BOSH SO2

    Basic Occupational Safety and Health Training is the basic foundation in delivering health and safety to your company, especially those that involve higher risk activities such as construction, manufacturing, mining, and transportation. BOSH Training can be provided by certified trainers and can vary in duration and content depending on the specific requirements of the organization or regulatory agency.

    COSH SO2

    Construction Occupational Safety and Health Training is a well-developed module customized for companies to enable concerned personnel(s) to identify hazards, and evaluate and correct them, so none of these unsafe conditions or unsafe acts turn into life-threatening situations that curb a company’s productive output.

    SPIES/ Safety Auditing Training

    Safety Program Implementation & Evaluation Systems or SPIES/SA training course is customized for your company to enable concerned personnel(s) to effectively implement the occupational safety and health management system and program and efficiently evaluate its effectiveness.

    LCM Advance OSH Training

    Loss Control Management (LCM) Advance OSH: Apply professional management skills to control loss from the risks of doing business and re-familiarize yourself with the Occupational Safety and Health Standards.

    WEM

    WEM training could refer to training related to Workplace Environment Management. Workplace Environment Management is the practice of creating and maintaining a safe, healthy, and productive work environment for employees.

    T3

    Train the Trainers typically involves a comprehensive curriculum that covers topics such as adult learning theory, training methods and techniques, instructional design, communication, presentation skills, feedback and evaluation.
